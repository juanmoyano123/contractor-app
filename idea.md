This B2B SaaS helps trades businesses (plumbing, electrical, roofing, HVAC, etc.) unlock new jobs by sharing referrals across trusted partners. Contractors list what they want and what they offer; after a job, they can instantly hand off related work. The platform tracks referrals, lead status, and payouts automatically, turning one-off favors into measurable, recurring revenue.

Operating in stealth with $0 ad spend, customers have generated $300K+ in new revenue while cutting marketing costs. Profitable, lean, and ready to scale via integrations and repeatable workflows—an ideal bolt-on for a trades software company, agency, or strategic buyer.
